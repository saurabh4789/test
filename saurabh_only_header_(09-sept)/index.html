Services provided by CRN are funded through contracts with the Arizona Health Care Cost Containment System (AHCCCS), Mercy Care and Steward Health Choice Arizona.

As of 2017, Crisis Response Network is a qualified Charitable tax Credit Organization in Arizona. The credit has been increased so that individuals can now give up to $400, and married couples can give up to $800. CRN’s assigned Qualified Charitable Organization Code (QCO Code) is: 20940.
